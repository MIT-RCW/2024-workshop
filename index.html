MIT RCW 2024

MIT Robotics & Climate Workshop

May 7th, 2024

On May 7th, 2024, the MIT Robotics & Climate Workshop (RCW) will be convening researchers from the robotics, climate, and sustainability communities for a day of presentations, panels, and discussions about how robotics research can support climate change science, mitigation, and adaptation efforts.
