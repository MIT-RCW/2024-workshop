MIT Robotics & Climate Workshop 2024

The MIT Robotics & Climate Workshop (RCW) will be convening researchers from the robotics, climate, and sustainability communities for a day of presentations, panels, and discussions about how robotics research can support climate change science, mitigation, and adaptation efforts.
